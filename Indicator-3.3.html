Indicator 3.3 - Number of regencies with a completed environmental and social risk assessment

Cumulative number of districts: Phase 1: 7 districts, Phase 2: 37 districts, Phase 3: 73 districts, Phase 4: 79 district, Phase 5: 59 districts (14 in Sumatera, 24 in Kalimantan and 21 in Java). As of September 2022, total cumulative number of 110 districts completed the assessment. As of end May 2023, total cumulative number of districts that completed the assessment increased to 141 districts (Implementation area of PTSL, PBKH, and PTPR). As of September 2023, due to the elimination of Gresik district from the Area of Interest of PTSL number of total districts under PTSL has changed to 140 districts. No updates as of February 2024.
